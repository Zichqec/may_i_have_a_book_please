The user takes on the role of a researcher, desperate to find the materials needed to complete their masterwork. Running out of options, they have located a library that claims to hold lost and forbidden knowledge... but they are not prepared for the strange library policies that await them inside.

The scenario is considered "complete" when the user obtains and reads 1 book.
